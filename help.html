Tiny RPN

Tiny RPN is a Reverse Polish Notation calculator, operated entirely with the keyboard. It displays the contents of the stack as you modify it (WYSIWYC — What You See Is What You Calculate :-)).

Instructions

  • To enter several numbers, separate them with spaces or commas, or press Enter/Return after each one. Negative numbers can be entered by using a leading underscore.

  • To perform an arithmetic operation, type its character (e.g. + for addition) in the entry box; instead of displaying the character, the stack will be updated. The arithmetic operations are:

    Operator Pops Pushes Description
    + 2 1 Add
    - 2 1 Subtract
    * 2 1 Multiply
    / 2 1 Divide
    % 2 1 Modulus
    ` 2 1 Raise to power
    & 2 1 Bitwise AND
    | 2 1 Bitwise OR
    ^ 2 1 Bitwise XOR
    ~ 1 1 Bitwise inverse

    • Tip: you don't have to enter a space between a number and one of the above operators. When you press the operator key, the number in the entry box will be pushed automatically.

  • To perform a named operation, type in its name followed by a space/comma/enter. Named commands are:

    Command Pops Pushes Description
    neg 1 1 Arithmetic negation
    and 2 1 Logical AND
    or 2 1 Logical OR
    not 2 1 Logical NOT
    pop 1 0 Pop back to entry box
    drop 1 0 Pop and discard
    drop2 2 0 Pop and discard twice
    swap 0 0 Exchange top two numbers
    dup 0 1 Duplicate top number
    dup2 0 2 Duplicate top number twice
    clear 0 N/A Clear entire stack
    undo 0 0 Undo previous operation
    iradix 1 0 Set input radix
    oradix 1 0 Set output radix
    rradix 0 0 Reset input/output radixes to 10
    noop 0 0 Do nothing

    plus these standard (to JavaScript, anyway) math functions:

    Function Pops Pushes Description
    abs 1 1 Absolute value
    acos 1 1 Arccosine of angle in radians
    asin 1 1 Arcsine of angle in radians
    atan 1 1 Arctangent of angle in radians
    atan2 2 1 Arctangent of quotient
    ceil 1 1 Least integer greater than
    cos 1 1 Cosine of angle in radians
    exp 1 1 Raise e to power
    floor 1 1 Greatest integer less than
    log 1 1 Natural logarithm
    max 2 1 Greater of two numbers
    min 2 1 Lesser of two numbers
    pow 2 1 Raise to power
    random 0 1 Random float in range [0,1)
    round 1 1 Round to nearest integer
    sin 1 1 Sine of angle in radians
    sqrt 1 1 Square root
    tan 1 1 Tangent of angle in radians

    and these constants:

    ConstantDescription
    EBase of natural logarithm
    LN2Natural logarithm of 2
    LN10Natural logarithm of 10
    LOG2EBase-2 log of e
    LOG10EBase-10 log of e
    PIπ
    SQRT1_2Square root of ½
    SQRT2Square root of 2

  • Some functions are available via control keys:

    KeyOperation
    Ctrl-Zundo
    Ctrl-Tswap (think "transpose")

  • Numbers can also be popped by pressing the Backspace key when the entry box is empty.

  • The popup can be closed with the Esc key.

Standalone Use

If you would like to set up an application shortcut in order to use Tiny RPN outside of your normal browser window, use this page: popup.html. (Chrome currently disables the "Create Application Shortcuts" menu item for extension pages, but running chrome --app=URL from the command line does work. Right-click on the link above to copy the URL.)
